Why join us?

Get Social

We have weekly coffee meetings to chat and have good time together. We also have a lot of activities. Sometimes we do gardening together.

Feel Healthy

We have free yoga and stretching classes twice a week. We also have a dancing class on Tuesdays. We go mountain hiking in the summer.

Be Creative

We have free weekly Arts and crafts class. We knit, crochet, make holidays related decorations, paint and draw, do mosaics, etc.
